What We Do

Our Practice Areas

01

Personal Injury

We represent accident victims in claims involving car crashes, slip-and-fall incidents, workplace injuries, and medical malpractice. Our contingency-fee model means you pay nothing unless we recover compensation on your behalf.

02

Family Law

From divorce and child custody arrangements to adoption and spousal support, our family law attorneys handle sensitive matters with the discretion, empathy, and legal precision your family deserves.

03

Criminal Defense

Facing criminal charges requires experienced, aggressive defense counsel. We protect your constitutional rights at every stage — from arraignment through trial — and work tirelessly to achieve the best possible outcome.

04

Business Law

We advise corporations, partnerships, and startups on entity formation, contract drafting, commercial disputes, mergers, and compliance matters. Sound legal counsel is the foundation of every successful business.

05

Real Estate Law

Our real estate attorneys handle residential and commercial transactions, title disputes, landlord-tenant conflicts, and property development matters with meticulous attention to protecting your investment.

06

Immigration Law

We guide individuals and families through visa applications, green card petitions, naturalization proceedings, and deportation defense — providing clear guidance through a complex and often intimidating process.

07

Estate Planning

Protect your legacy and your loved ones with comprehensive wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and probate administration. We craft estate plans that reflect your precise wishes and minimize unnecessary tax liability.
